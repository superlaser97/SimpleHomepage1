Quick Tips & Features

Reorder Widgets

Easily arrange shortcuts and categories to fit your workflow. Your layout is saved automatically!

  • Reorder Categories & Shortcuts: Click and drag any category header to change its vertical order.
  • Move Shortcuts: Simply drag a shortcut from one group and drop it into another.

Quick Edits & Management

  • The page title is editable! Just click on it directly.
  • Click directly on any Note or Reminder card to open its editor.
  • Hover over a category title to reveal its edit button.
  • In the shortcut editor, hover over category or custom icon tags to find their management controls.

Personalize Your Icons

Make the dashboard truly yours by uploading custom icons for your shortcuts.

  • In the shortcut editor, use the Upload Icons button to select your own SVG files.
  • Hover over a custom icon in the selector to reveal a button to delete it.
  • For best results, use simple, single-color SVGs. The app will automatically color them to match your theme.

Powerful Real-time Search

The search bar is a dynamic filter for your active tab.

  • Press / to focus the search bar from anywhere.
  • As you type, the content on the active tab (Shortcuts, Notes, or Reminders) will be filtered instantly.
  • Search works on shortcut names, categories, note titles, note content, and reminder messages.

Pro-Level Note Taking

Your notes support simple markdown. Try these in the note editor:

  • Start a line with `##` for a subheading.
  • Start a line with `-` or `*` for a bullet point.
  • Separate paragraphs with a blank line.

Advanced Reminders

Set up complex repeating reminders.

  • Repeat every N days, weeks, months, or years.
  • Set reminders to end on a specific date or after a number of occurrences.
  • Triggered reminders will make the Reminders tab glow until you dismiss them.

Your Data, Your Control

Use the Import / Export buttons in Settings to save a backup or move your setup to another computer.

Use the button to reset everything to default.

Display & Theme

Use the controls in the top-right Settings menu to customize your view.

  • Toggle between Light and Dark themes.
  • Switch between Compact and Comfortable display styles for information density.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Navigate and work faster with these keys:

  • Press / at any time to instantly focus the search bar.
  • Press Esc to close any open modal window.
